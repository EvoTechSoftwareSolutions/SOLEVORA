At SoleVora, we believe that luxury extends beyond the physical product. It encompasses the entire experience, including how we handle your personal information. This policy outlines our commitment to your privacy.

When you interact with SoleVora, we collect information that helps us deliver a premium shopping experience. This includes personal identifiers such as your name, email address, shipping and billing addresses, and phone number when you create an account or place an order.

We also collect behavioral information through our encrypted payment gateway, automated analytics, and engagement signals used to enhance every aspect of your experience, from recommendations to service quality.

Your information powers a tailored shopping journey. We use it to process orders, personalize product suggestions, and communicate your order status with speed and clarity.

Beyond fulfillment, we analyze shopping behavior to improve product selection, streamline navigation, and craft meaningful updates while preserving user trust.

03

Cookies & Tracking

Our platform uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your browsing preferences, maintain secure sessions, and optimize checkout speed.

Analytical and performance cookies help us understand trends, improve functionality, and deliver a smoother and more relevant experience throughout your journey.

04

Third-Party Services

To provide a seamless shopping experience, we collaborate with select premium partners for payments, logistics, product insights, and technical support.

Every service provider relationship is governed by strict confidentiality agreements and audited security requirements to ensure your data remains protected.

05

Your Rights

You maintain control over your personal data. You have the right to access, correct, transfer, or request removal of your information at any time in accordance with applicable regulations.

If you wish to exercise any right under this policy, our support team can assist you through your account settings or customer service channels.

06

Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security architecture to protect your information, including transport encryption, secure storage, and integrity controls.

Our systems are continuously reviewed to maintain high standards of digital protection and operational resilience.
